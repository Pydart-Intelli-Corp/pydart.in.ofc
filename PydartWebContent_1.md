## FIRST DEPLOYMENT PLATFORM

### ALTRAS-1
**Advanced Linear Transferring & Reporting Automated System**

ALTRAS-1 is an Al-enabled robotic patient transfer platform designed to eliminate manual patient lifting and improve safety for both patients and healthcare professionals. Traditional patient transfers rely heavily on manual lifting, which exposes healthcare workers to injuries and increases the risk of patient mishandling. ALTRAS-1 transforms this process through automated robotic transfer technology combined with real-time medical monitoring. This system introduces intelligent automation into hospital patient mobility operations.

### ALTRAS-1 CORE CAPABILITIES

**Automated Vertical Lift**
Motorized lift system enabling bed alignment and controlled height adjustment.

**Automated Horizontal Transfer**
Smooth bed-to-bed transfer system that eliminates the need for manual lifting.

**Al-Assisted Transfer Control**
Smart algorithms assist with weight distribution and safety monitoring during transfers. Features include:
* load sensing
* transfer monitoring
* fall detection support

**Integrated Medical Monitoring**
ALTRAS-1 integrates patient monitoring systems that provide real-time medical insights. Monitoring capabilities include:
* ECG
* $SpO_{2}$ monitoring
* Blood pressure measurement
* real-time alert system

These capabilities transform ALTRAS-1 from a mechanical device into an intelligent healthcare robotics platform.
