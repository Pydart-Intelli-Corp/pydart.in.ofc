# ============================
# HOW IT WORKS - ZRV
# ============================

## The Problem with Traditional Transfers

Traditional patient transfer causes friction, shear forces, and physical strain, leading to patient discomfort and caregiver injuries. In Indian hospitals with high patient loads and limited staff, each nurse may perform 15-20 transfers per shift.

---

## Zero Relative Velocity Explained

ZRV ensures that as the transfer board extends, the belt surface moves in perfect synchronization. This keeps the patient stationary relative to the bed, eliminating friction and shear forces - a breakthrough developed entirely in India.

---

## Why ZRV Matters
- Prevents skin injuries - critical for elderly patients
- Improves comfort - maintains patient dignity
- Enables safe automation - single-operator transfers
- Reduces transfer time by 40% - improving hospital efficiency
